    Raising Perfect Puppies

    We take pride in raising best quality puppies of such breeds as Cavapoo, Mini Poodle, Mini Labradoodle with excellent health, great temperaments and soft coats.
    Here are the steps we take to create the best litters and raise finest pups:

    • Parents are vet checked and found clean of any illnesses and genetic disorders.
    • Puppies are born in our home and are supervized, checked and weighted daily, ensuring proper development.
    • Starting from day two each puppy is going through Early Neurological Stimulation (ENS) excercises which includes: tackle stimulation, Head Held Erect, Head Pointed Down, Supine Position, Thermal Stimulation.
    • At the age of two weeks puppies are slowly introduced to noises, environments, kids, and other house pets.
    • Puppies have variety of toys of different sizes, textures and forms to help with teething and entertainment.

    Each puppy will be going home with their goodie bag which includes Vet Records and Vaccination, Birth Certificate with the puppy paw print and Registration(if available), a small bag of food they are currently eat, their favorite toy and a blanket with the litter scent to help with smooth transition to a new family.

    Breed Characteristics

    Cavapoo

    One of the best small family god breed. The size, coloring, and coat texture of Cavapoo make them look like a teddy bear, and be adored by small kids.

    • Hypoallergenic.
    • Affectionate, outgoing and playful.
    • Ineligent, easy to train.
    • Low maintnance dog.

    They are also known as Cavapoodle, Cavadoodle, and Cavoodle.

    Breed Characteristics

    Mini Labradoodle

    A cross between Labrador Retriever and Mini Poodle. Loving, adorable and fathful family dog breed.

    • Friendly, intelligent, and social.
    • Quick learners.
    • Suitable as assistance and therapy dogs.
    • Hypoallergenic.

    Our Mini Labradoodles will weight 28-35lbs when fully grown, which makes them perfect dog for families with small kids.

    Breed Characteristics

    Miniature Poodle

    Beneath the curly, hypoallergenic coat is an elegant athlete and companion for all reasons and seasons.

    • One of the smartest dogs in the world. Highly trainable.
    • Hypoallergenic.
    • Good with young children.
    • Adventurous and love to go on a hike, walk, and swim.
    • So many grooming styles to choose from!

    Reserve early if you want your puppy's tail natural length, othervise it will be docked according to breed requirements.
